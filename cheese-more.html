CHEESE HAUS

Complete rebrand for a small business out of Mildmay, Ontario. This project is one of the bigger ones that I have done, involving many hours of sketches, consultation, and far too long in Illustrator.

Lots of the companies in smaller towns are starting to update their brands and logos in order to keep up with the times, this is an example of that. I wanted to give them something modern, but not to diffuse their family-friendly, hometown feel.

I kept the mouse in their logo, and I updated the oranges to be more bright and welcoming. I changed them over from a serif to a sans serif font, which on itâ€™s own really modernized their brand. I ended up with something very simple and modern, but that mouse adds some interest and fun into the brand.
